My Design Process

  1. Discover

    Once you place your order, we begin working together. We will read the outline of your project and take the time to understand the objectives of your site, your target users, and how they will interact with your site.

  2. Plan

    Information architecture is key in every web project. We’ll start by creating a wireframe that outlines your project concept. This phase will result in a detailed plan for your site or page structure.

  3. Execute

    This is where the magic happens! I will create a functional and top-notch design based on the planned structure. We’ll coordinate meetings with you so that you can review the design and give feedback.

  4. Deliver

    Based on your feedback and our expertise, I'll make all changes needed and make sure you are completely satisfied. Once the project is complete, I will deliver to you the final design and all of the necessary assets.
